Help Page - Siwalaxy

How can we assist you?

1. How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent via email.

2. How do I update my personal information?

Go to your profile from the main dashboard of the application.

3. How can I contact the Siwalaxy team?

You can reach us at: siwalaxy@gmail.com.

4. How do I change my profile picture?

Go to your account settings and select "Change Profile Picture."

5. What should I do if I encounter a technical issue?

If you experience a technical problem, contact our team at siwalaxy@gmail.com or visit our help center.

6. How do I install the application on my phone?

Siwalaxy is a web application and can be installed on your phone like a regular app using Progressive Web App (PWA) technology.

Follow these steps to install Siwalaxy on your phone:

  1. On Android (Google Chrome):

    1. Open the application in Google Chrome.
    2. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right corner.
    3. Select "Add to Home screen" and confirm.
  2. On iPhone (Safari):

    1. Open the application in Safari.
    2. Tap the Share button (icon at the bottom).
    3. Select "Add to Home Screen" and confirm.
    4. An icon will appear on your home screen, allowing you to use Siwalaxy like a mobile app.
